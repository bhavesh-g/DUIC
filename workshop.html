Our Clubs And Workshops

Recursion - CLUB

The club Recursion, is the central point where convergence of all technical and scientific endeavors of the students is materialized. This technical club of the department is working in new way by which we try to bridges the gap between theory knowledge and practical application development. A number of extracurricular activities held in the department around the year will be notified to the students by the Recursion( Android App).

Android Programming

Recently, a workshop on “Android Programming” was conducted in department with live android application development.A huge response of students was recorded from 1st to 3rd year students of CSE and IT. As per recent requirements of software companies, our department is adopting new technology based syllabus and conducted many departmental level courses in parallel to other short term courses. Codeshastra - CLUB

It is a code club means growing your coding skills together and sharing your knowledge and skills at problem solving with others. In this a team of students from third or final year are working a trainer for first and second year students to make them technically sound in computer languages. With the help of these teams department is running programming language based doubt classes on Saturday, Sunday and other days after 5 PM during session. A link for students registration: Codeshastra

Hands-on Workshop

Electronic Circuits Design on PCB and Sensors Interfacing with Arduino

organized for engineering students under the banner of Pt. DeenDayalUpadhaya Innovation and Incubation Centre (DUIC) in our university. The aim of the workshops was to provide practical hands-on training to the engineering students in order to sharpen their technical skills and hence promote students towards innovation. Dr. Deepak Kedia, Director, Innovation Centre was the Co-ordinator of both the workshops.The workshops were sponsored by World Bank Project TEQIP-III of the university. Visit Gallery
