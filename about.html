about DUIC

Pandit Deen dayal Upaadhyay Incubation Center.An autonoumous body right in the heart of Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technoly, Hisar.We aim to provide startup friendly environment to our students.Our students will be job producers rather than job seekers.

Aims

The Start-up Cell in the university has recently been constituted as per guidelines received from NPIU.Recently, Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Innovation and Incubation Centre has also been established in the University. The future action plan of our university to promote and support Student Innovations and Start-up Activities is
1. Creating dedicated infrastructure for innovation and start-up activites.
2. Budgetary support for students’ innovative ideas.
3. Organizing Workshops / Short term programs on Innovation, Start-up and Entrepreneurship.
4. MoU / Collaboration with expert institute/industry/organization to support student innovators/start-ups.
5. Aim for a full fledged and functional Incubation Centre in the University.

Objectives

1. To Develop a Critical Mass of Motivated Students & Faculties with Entrepreneurial Orientation & Skill
2. To Build Infrastructure Support for Innovation & Early Stage Enterprise development and Enabling Access to Resource & Facilities at Institute
3. To Enhance In-House Competency Development to Serve Potential and Early Stage Entrepreneurs and Student Innovators at the Institute.
4. To Strengthen the Inter Department and Inter-Institutional linkage, Incubators and Other Ecosystem Enablers at Different Levels.

Goals

Creating a vibrant and dynamic Startup Ecosystem in Technical Institutions by playing a role of pre-incubator to promote, facilitate support system to innovative and entrepreneurial students and faculties to convert their innovative ideas/problems to tech-solution with a feasible business model stage. Role of Pre-Incubator is to connect various student clubs (Idea clubs, Innovation Clubs, Start-up Clubs) to come up with tech solutions for the problems from Industry, Society, and Market to generate Ideas/Proof of Concepts (PoCs) and helping them to get converted to Prototypes and mentor them to develop business models ready. Therefore, creating a strong pipeline of quality and quantity tech based potential start-ups for incubators industry to take further.
