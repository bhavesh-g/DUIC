How to start with STARTUPs

Idea

Don't wait for an idea to strike, just look around yourself and try to solve problems.

Validation

Check the ground reality of your thoughts, test it and discuss it with right people.

Team

Get some team members to work on your idea, share your strategy with them and listen to their views on it.

Rough Sketch

Write down things, make simplest prototype, improve it. Prepare a presentation.

Submit

Come to us with all steps completed, our panel will review it and further details will be given.
